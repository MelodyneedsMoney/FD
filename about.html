Fukushima Discrepancy

Fukushima Discrepancy is a project about the dynamic relationship amongst the media news, the public discussion, and the event of Japan Fukushima treated water.
It also aims at exploring the potential possibility of endangering marine life by political-related public opinion.
An absurd and playful way of presentation is designed to display how this trending environment topic is changing in the public.
The original data is scraped from the replies under different news on Twitter.

Through sentimental analysis of each set of replies, the processed data is gathered.
The visualization of data is applied in generative sketches.
The process of the mutation of the hand-drawn style marine creatures can be seen when the mouse moves to the animations, which include underlying meanings related to the nuclear-treated water event.
There is a gallery collecting all the mutant creatures, which links to the original Twitter news pages.
